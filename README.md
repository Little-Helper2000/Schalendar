Yes! All your data stays locally on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information.

Simply add your classes or work shifts to the calendar. Schalendar automatically tracks your attendance and absences.

Yes! You can add classes, work shifts, or any recurring commitments to your calendar.

### How do I set goals?
Use the built-in notes and checklist section to track your goals and tasks.
